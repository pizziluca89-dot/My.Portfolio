Professional Roles

Psychotherapist

Private Practitioner | 2017 - Present

I work as a CBT psychotherapist in private practice, supporting individuals and couples in their journey of personal and relational growth.

Lecturer

Freelance | 2020 - Present

I have taught training courses for mental health professionals, focusing on techniques and approaches grounded in scientific evidence. Teaching roles include:
- Sigmund Freud University of Milan, in digital psychology and psychotherapy.
- University of Pavia, Master’s program in Psychology of Aging.

Coordinator for RSA Aperta Program

Segesta Gestioni S.r.l. | 2019 - 2023

I worked as a CBT psychotherapist within the “RSA Aperta” service, providing psychological support and promoting well-being among elderly residents and their families.

Career

Postgraduate School in CBT Psychotherapy

Cognitive Psychotherapy and Research Institute | 2018 - 2021

I completed my specialization in Cognitive-Behavioral Psychotherapy at the “Cognitive Psychotherapy and Research Institute” in Milan, developing advanced skills in the application of evidence-based therapeutic techniques.

Advanced Training in OCD Treatment

Ipsico | 2025

I completed an advanced training program in the treatment of Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) at Ipsico, under the scientific direction of Dr. Gabriele Melli, gaining specialized expertise in therapeutic interventions for this disorder.

Master’s in Full-Stack Development

Start2impact | 2025 - Ongoing

I am currently pursuing a Master’s in Full-Stack Development at Start2impact, acquiring advanced technical skills in building complete web applications, from front-end design to back-end management.

Master’s in Psychology of Aging

University of Pavia | 2019 - 2020

I earned a Master’s degree in Psychology of Aging from the University of Pavia, focusing on the psychological well-being of older adults, caregivers, and multidisciplinary teams, as well as effective intervention strategies in this field.

Master’s in Emergency Psychology

LR Psicologia | 2016 - 2017

I completed a Master’s in Emergency Psychology at LR Psicologia, gaining specialized skills in psychological intervention during crises and emergencies, including natural disasters, accidents, and other traumatic events.
